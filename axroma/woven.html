Yarn-dyed

Our yarn dyed woven take advantage of all synthetic fibers that are great weathering performance, and are colored firstly by different yarn dyeing process to generate beautiful coloreffects, then are woven with different proportion of warps & wefts for chambray textures, multi-colored stripes, Scottish tartan plaid, polka dots, or geometrics, with specially twisted or texturized fancy yarns for spectacular tropical floral, classic damask or paisley. Yarn dyed woven is the finest form of performance textiles for outdoor and residential.


Solution-dyed

Add master color batch or dyesstuff to the liquid fiber solution BEFORE the fibers are extruded. This allows the color all the way through the fiber once it is extruded. This process ensures that colors completely permeate the fiber and enables the yarn to stand up to the elements better than yarn-dyed or piece-dyed fabrics.

Oure woven fabrics made of 100% solution-dyed polyester and solution-dyed polypropylene are the best choice for outdoor environment as well as indoor home. Besides great fade resistant, they both process features of easy cleaning and low maintenance as solution-dyed fibers can normally be cleaned with a mild bleach solution and do not support mildewgrowth. The solution-dyed fabrics is deemed as the future of the performance fabrics and will deserve our continuous investment and fixation to grow and expand.
